Pork Sausage

Pork Sausage is made by grinding pork meat with fat, salt and other seasononings (as well as preservative and fillers in some cases).Pork sausage can be fresh (like Italian sausage), cooked (like hot dogs); smoked (like Andouille); or cured and dried (like salami).

Pumpkin Soup

Pumpkin soup is a usually 'bound' soup made from a purée of pumpkin. It is made by combining the meat of a blended pumpkin with broth or stock. It can be served hot or cold.
